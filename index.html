Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start blogging ? You can start a blog today by taking these steps:
  1. Know the purpose of your blog
  2. Prepare some content/posts
  3. Know your audience
  4. Learn some HTML, CSS and JS
  5. Purchase a domain
  6. Take a free or paid hosting
    7. Know More...
How can I earn money from blogging ? It is possible to earn money from blog. You can earn money from blogs by placing ads and affilliate liks on your blog post. People do visit to your blog and if they are intersted in that advertisement as they click you will be paid. The most preferable monitisation company is Google Adsense. If you want to know more about earning from blogs Click here
Do I need certificate to start blogging ? No, there is no need to get certified for writing blogs or there is no need of any degree or certificate to start your carrier in blogging field. Only just by learning some basic HTML, CSS and JavaScript you will be able to design blogs. You need to be good at English for making dashing and speaking content. If you can spend more you can also skip these languages. If don't want to stuck with web programming you can try CMS like WordPress, Wix, Blogger etc.
Do bloggers make money ? Yes, bloggers makes money by placing third party ads like Google Adsense and affilliate links like Amazon on their posts for the purpose of revenue generation. Once someone clicks that ads or purchased something form the affilliate link the owner of that blog will be paid.
How much a blogger can earn? On an average, a typical blogger earns around $300 – $400 (20,000 - 29,000 INR approx.) a month. However, if the blogger is more experienced, then he/she can earn up to $3000+ also. Celebrity bloggers in India earn between $20,000 and $30,000(14 - 21 lakhs approx.) a month. Most of the bloggers are average bloggers who are earning $500 – $1000.
