After purchasing a free domain from freenom you need to connect it to blogger. To connect freenom domain with blogger you need to do some blogger setting and some setting on freenom website. Here you will learn connecting freenom account with blogger step by step. Follow all the steps given below:
    
 



  

    
STEP: 01
Firstly open setting and go to Basic then click on Setup third party URL for your blog to add custom domain. A box will open and then enter your freenom domain there. Click on save. 

      
     

    
STEP: 02
Now the most important point is to copy these four IDs.
      

        
    

    
STEP: 03
Open freenom and go to "My domains".
      

        
      

     

    
STEP: 04
Now go to "Manage Domains". 


    
STEP: 05
Click on "Management Tools". 


    
STEP: 06
Click on "Nameservers". 


    
STEP: 07
Select "Use default nameservers". 


    
STEP: 08 
Click on "Manage Freenom DNS". 

      
    

    
STEP: 09
Click on more record. 


    
STEP: 10
Fill four records as it is.
             

               3600  A 216.239.32.21 

               3600  A 216.239.34.21 

               3600  A 216.239.36.21 

               3600  A 216.239.38.21 

             
Please do not use @ if found some error 


    
STEP: 11
Add two more records as stated in "Step 02". Click save changes. 


    
STEP: 12
Your Domain will be connected with your blogger within 2-4 hours or in a minute
